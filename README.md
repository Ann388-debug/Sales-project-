Project Overview

My first end-to-end sales analytics project — where my journey into data analytics began. Using Excel, SQL, and Tableau, I cleaned, transformed, queried, and visualized sales data, built dashboards and pivot tables, and delivered key insights with business recommendations.

This project focuses on analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, payment methods, and regional trends to uncover actionable business insights.

 Key Insights

 Regional Performance
- Mombasa emerged as the best-performing region in terms of sales.
- Kisumu recorded the lowest sales performance among all regions analyzed.

Product Performance
- Laptops were the most sold product category, showing strong customer demand.

 Customer Payment Behavior
- Most payments were made using cards, suggesting that a large portion of customers are business professionals or digitally comfortable consumers.

Customer Loyalty
- The top 5 customers accounted for 3,000,651 in total sales revenue.
- This suggests strong customer loyalty and satisfaction with the services offered.

 Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing efforts and promotions in Kisumu to improve regional performance.
- Maintain strong inventory management for laptops due to high demand.
- Introduce loyalty programs targeting repeat high-value customers.
- Expand digital and card payment options to improve customer convenience.
